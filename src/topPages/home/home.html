Kathak

Born and developed in Northen India, it is a form of Indian classical dance. Katha means story and Katthika means she who tells a story and thus the name Kathak. Katha kahe so Kathk means she who tells a story is Kathak.

The beauty of Kathak is that it is the only form of Indian classical dance that represents a strong synergy between Hindu and Muslim culture.
